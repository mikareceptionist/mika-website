SMS Notification Terms

Mika Technologies, LLC  ·  Last updated: June 29, 2026

Mika sends SMS text message notifications to law firm subscribers after each call. This page describes what those messages contain, how you opt in, how message frequency is determined, and how to opt out at any time.

What These Messages Are

When Mika answers a call on your firm's behalf, you receive an immediate SMS notification containing:

  • The caller's name and phone number
  • A brief summary of the caller's legal matter
  • A link to the full call transcript and intake details in your dashboard

These are transactional service notifications — not marketing messages. They are sent only when your Mika assistant completes a call.

How You Opt In

SMS notifications are optional. You opt in during account setup by checking the SMS opt-in checkbox and entering your mobile phone number. You will not receive SMS messages unless you explicitly check this box.

Message Frequency

Message frequency depends on your call volume. You will receive one SMS per completed call that Mika handles — typically 1–30 messages per day depending on your firm's incoming call volume. During periods of high call volume, you may receive more messages. You can always mute notifications or adjust settings from your Mika dashboard.

Message and Data Rates

Standard message and data rates from your wireless carrier may apply. Mika does not charge separately for SMS notifications — they are included in your subscription. Check with your carrier if you have questions about your messaging plan.

How to Opt Out

You can opt out of SMS notifications at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any Mika text message. You will receive one confirmation message and no further SMS notifications.
  • Unchecking the SMS notification setting in your Mika account dashboard under Account Settings.
  • Contacting support at mika@callmika.com.

After opting out, you will continue to receive call summaries by email (email notifications are required for the service to function).

How to Get Help

Reply HELP to any Mika SMS message, or contact us at mika@callmika.com. We respond to support requests within one business day.

Carriers

Mika SMS notifications are delivered through Twilio and are compatible with all major U.S. wireless carriers including AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, and others. Carrier is not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Privacy

We will not share your mobile phone number with third parties for their marketing purposes. Your number is used solely to deliver Mika call notifications. See our Privacy Policy for complete details on how we handle your data.

Questions about SMS notifications? Contact us at mika@callmika.com.
