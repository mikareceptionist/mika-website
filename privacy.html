Privacy Policy

Mika Technologies, LLC  ·  Last updated: June 29, 2026

Mika Technologies, LLC ("Mika," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Mika AI Receptionist service at app.callmika.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and your rights regarding your data. By using our service, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in three ways: information you provide directly, information generated through your use of the service, and information from callers who reach your firm through Mika.

  • Account information: Your name, email address, law firm name, practice areas, and notification preferences when you create an account.
  • Contact information: Phone numbers you register for notifications or call forwarding.
  • Call data: Recordings, transcripts, and AI-generated summaries of calls Mika handles on your behalf. Caller names, phone numbers, matter types, and other intake information collected during calls.
  • Payment information: Billing details processed through Stripe. We do not store full card numbers — payment data is handled directly by Stripe.
  • Usage data: Call volume, session logs, and feature usage to operate and improve the service.
  • Communications: Emails or support messages you send us.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the Mika AI receptionist service — answering calls, conducting intake, and sending you summaries.
  • To send email and SMS notifications about calls handled by Mika.
  • To process payments and manage your subscription.
  • To configure and personalize your Mika assistant (firm name, practice areas, voice, etc.).
  • To improve our service, detect abuse, and ensure security.
  • To send transactional emails (receipts, account alerts, product updates).
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Third-Party Services We Use

We share data with the following third-party providers to operate the service. Each provider has its own privacy policy governing their use of data.

  • Vapi — AI call processing and voice agent infrastructure. Call audio and transcript data passes through Vapi's systems.
  • Twilio — Telephony and SMS delivery. Phone numbers, call routing, and SMS notifications are processed through Twilio.
  • ElevenLabs — Voice synthesis. Mika's voice is generated through ElevenLabs' text-to-speech platform.
  • Anthropic — AI language model used for website crawling during onboarding and call analysis.
  • Stripe — Payment processing and subscription billing. Stripe stores and processes all payment card data.
  • Resend — Transactional email delivery (call summaries, account notifications, auth emails).
  • Supabase — Database hosting and authentication. Your account data and call records are stored in Supabase's infrastructure.
  • Vercel — Application hosting for app.callmika.com.

We do not sell your personal information or your callers' information to third parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data and call records for the duration of your subscription, plus a reasonable backup period of up to 90 days after account closure. You may request earlier deletion of your data by contacting us at mika@callmika.com.

Call recordings and transcripts are retained for as long as your account is active. You can delete individual call records from your dashboard at any time.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Export your call data from the Mika dashboard.
  • Opt out of marketing communications (transactional service emails are required for operation).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at mika@callmika.com. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Caller Data

When callers reach your firm through Mika, their name, phone number, and intake information are collected as part of the service. This data is stored in your account and is your responsibility to handle in accordance with your own privacy obligations to your clients. Mika does not use caller data for any purpose other than providing the service to you.

7. SMS Communications

If you opt in to SMS notifications, Mika will send you text message summaries after each completed call. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any Mika text message. For full SMS terms, see our SMS Terms page.

8. Cookies and Analytics

Our marketing website (callmika.com) uses standard analytics tools and may use cookies to understand site traffic and improve the user experience. Our application (app.callmika.com) uses session cookies required for authentication. We do not use third-party advertising cookies on the application.

9. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS), secure database hosting, and access controls. No system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

10. Children's Privacy

Mika is a business service not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or by posting a notice at app.callmika.com. Continued use of the service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Contact us at mika@callmika.com.
